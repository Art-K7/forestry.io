Mission

At Forestry.io, we believe the web is one of humanity’s greatest achievements. Our mission is to help people build a better web.

Story

Our co-founders Scott (CEO) and Jordan (CTO) have been building websites and tools for the web for over a decade. In early 2016, we created Forestry.io to help us further our mission. Shortly after, we launched Forestry CMS, hired our first developer and joined Techstars NYC. Since then, we’ve grown our team to 10+ people, have created a product we’re very proud of and have sold our enterprise solution to Fortune 500 companies.

Location

We’re founded and headquartered in the small town of Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island, Canada. The bulk of our team works out of our office here, but our first hire, and nearly 50% of our staff work remotely from all over North America and Europe.

Team

We’re a group of techie, passionate and kind people who love working together to build great things for the web. We thrive on autonomy and collaboration, and enjoy utilizing the diverse perspectives of our team members to solve hard problems.
